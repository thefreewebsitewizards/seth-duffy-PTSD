Meet Seth Duffy — The PTSD Dude

Wellness Coach | Breathwork Specialist | Stress & Relationship Mentor

Seth Duffy, known as The PTSD Dude on Instagram @theptsddude, is a certified Wellness and Breathwork Coach dedicated to helping individuals reclaim balance, resilience, and joy. With over a decade of experience in fitness, stress management, and relationship coaching, Seth blends holistic wellness with practical tools that make change sustainable.

Holding a Bachelor's in Behavioral Psychology from West Virginia University and a Master's from Grand Canyon University, Seth brings scientific insight and grounded compassion. He is also a certified life and breathwork coach through the Life Design Project (LDP), integrating psychological understanding, coaching frameworks, and emotional intelligence into his work.

Whether clients are overcoming burnout, navigating relationships, or transforming habits, Seth’s approach bridges science-backed techniques with mindfulness and breathwork to build clarity, strength, and authentic self-growth.

He works both virtually and in person across the U.S. His mission: help people exhale stress, inhale strength, and live with purpose every single day.
